About Me

In October of 2022, I was looking for a new skill to help me in finding a new career that I was passionate about. As I was searching through catalogs of online courses I could take in my free time, I came across sites that taught coding. The more I looked into it, the more I was convinced that I could learn this skill which seemed to be more relevant now more than ever. And so began my journey to learn coding and become a Web Developer.


I graduated from the University of California Berkeley in 2005 with a degree in Psychology. Out of college, I decided to take a job working for an airline, which fulfilled my desire to travel and see the world. I only meant to stay in the job for about two to three years, but I enjoyed the job so much I stayed for more than a decade. It wasn't only the travel perks, but the people I worked with and the everyday challenges that strengthened my skills. I learned to work as part of a team, I learned to become resourceful in solving problems, and I learned that even though I was quiet and reserved, I could be a strong leader.

Currently I am working as a Service Aide for the City and County of San Francisco. I enjoy the work and the benefits, but I am always looking for ways to improve myself and learn skills that will advance my career.
